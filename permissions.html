Tools for...

Shareyourpaper.org can work with any repository. Our job is to make it easy for authors to do a legal deposit, and then get the files to you as conveniently as possible.

Supported by leaders at

Saves Time

  • No permissions checking: we built a system that automates permissions checking.
  • No version checking: we check every file automatically, giving authors instant feedback.
  • No juggling files: authors can easily drag and drop files into your repository, not your inbox.
  • No metadata hunting: we’ll collect openly available metadata for your deposits.
  • No Q&A our simple interface provides authors everything they need to know.

Improve Services

  • Easy deposit = more deposits: by making deposit drag and drop, with instant gratification, expect more deposits!
  • Empowers champions: with simple links directly to deposit that always make work more open.
  • Build Relationships: we’ll notify you in real time when authors need help.
  • Embed deposit anywhere: from your website, to promotion forms, and more.

Advances Mission

  • More open scholarship: by making archiving not just the right thing, but the easy thing.
  • Reinvest in outreach: with the time saved and powerful new tools.
  • Without a repository: leverage your reach to get deposits, into Zenodo, while still building your institutions visibility.

Easy to Install & Maintain

  • On your website: with a familiar URL and trustworthy branding.
  • Works with: DSpace, bpress, and more.
  • Easy set up: no migrations needed, get set up in 30 minutes without your tech team.
  • One click pilot: to help you quickly try something new, and see how it goes.
  • No maintenance: we handle upgrades and hosting.

For change, not for profit

By making deposit simple, and enabling every repository to upgrade easily, together we can transform self-archiving, in process and perception, to make dramatically more content open access in an equitable way. It’s free, so anyone can use it, but we need your leadership to sustain it.
