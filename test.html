This is a landing page for syp testing. It has some quick links to demo the different kinds of shareyourpaper responses and actions.

SYP can have quite a few different configs so the below dois can bring different responses. It's good to test at least two configs:

  • syp homepage
  • library default

  • 1. Start a redeposit with 10.1186/1758-2946-3-47 then choose "still want it in scholarworks" to see the redeposit flow.
  • 2. A DOI that shows full info about not submitting publisher PDF etc: 10.1126/scitranslmed.3001922
  • 3. A DOI where publisher PDF would be allowed: 10.1063/1.4937158
  • 4. A DOI where you can't share anything openly 10.15650/hebruniocollannu.89.2018.0221
  • 5. Here is an example of a direct linkout: https://josephmcarthur.github.io/playground/syp-dev?doi=10.1073/pnas.1905762116
  • 6. A DOI where there is an embargo & AAM override 10.1086/704224
