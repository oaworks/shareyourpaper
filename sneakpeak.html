About

Shareyourpaper.org is the simplest way to legally make your paper freely available on sites like Google Scholar and Web of Science to get a citation bump.

If the DOI give you a paywalled paper: doi.org/10.1126/scitranslmed.3008973

We help you free it: shareyourpaper.org/10.1126/scitranslmed.3008973

We're experts, and designed this with libraries. Your library can even get a special version just for your institution!

How it works

Shareyourpaper.org is open source and built by an academia-owned non-profit to make it easy, fast, and safe to freely share your paper. We're funded by leading libraries and foundations, so we won't sell your work, your data, or send you endless emails.

Behind the scenes, shareyourpaper.org works by gathering up information about your paper from the journal, checking copyright details, and seeing if it's already freely available. We then figure out the simplest way you can share it, guide you through it, and double check what you share is okay. Then it gets put in a general repository that preserves your work, and makes it discoverable. Neat, right?

Our main data sources are Crossref, Microsoft Academic, Unpaywall, Europe PMC, and Open Access Button's Permissions Database. All work is stored in Zenodo.

It's all powered by a free and open API you can use to build even cooler stuff.

If you find questions or see a problem, email us at hello@openaccessbutton.org.
